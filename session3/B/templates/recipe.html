Caipirinha

Want a fun way to celebrate the World Cup with the national cocktail? Make Caipirinha, made with lime, sugar, ice, and cachaça, which is a sugarcane-derived product. The alcohol results from the fermentation of the sugarcane juice that is distilled. Here is the recipe:

Directions
  1. In a small glass, add lime and honey.
  2. Using a pestle or a wooden spoon gently mash the lime and the honey together. Do not squeeze the lime too much.
  3. Add ice and the cachaça. Enjoy!
